hello, I’m Nezzie

The calm behind the celebration

A decade of planning weddings across the Carolinas, and a belief that the best days are the ones you actually get to enjoy.

My Story

It started with a love of bringing people together.

I planned my first wedding more than ten years ago and never looked back. What began as a gift for a friend became a calling: building celebrations where every detail feels intentional and every guest feels held.

Since then, I’ve had the honor of planning over 150 weddings — from intimate vineyard dinners to grand ballroom evenings — always with the same conviction. A wedding is not a checklist. It’s a once-in-a-lifetime story, and it deserves to be told beautifully.

Today, Nezzie Events is a boutique studio by design. I take on a limited number of couples each year so that yours never feels like one of many.

What I Believe

A planning philosophy

✻

Stress-free, always

Rooted in the Carolinas

Charlotte is home — and our advantage.

From The Duke Mansion to Lake Norman estates, mountain venues in Asheville, and hidden garden gems across the Piedmont, I know the Carolinas’ most beautiful spaces and the people who make them shine.

Those relationships mean trusted vendors, smoother logistics, and doors that open a little more easily — all in service of your day.

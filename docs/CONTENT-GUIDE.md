## Brand Voice

**Warm, calm, quietly confident, editorial.** Speaks like a trusted friend who happens to be an expert. Never salesy, never frantic, never gimmicky.

- **Do:** short, graceful sentences; sensory and emotional language; first-person ("I" / "we") for the planner; reassurance ("you can stay present").
- **Don't:** exclamation overload, jargon, hard-sell urgency, clichés like "dream team" or "your special day" overused.

### Messaging pillars (weave these throughout)
1. **Stress-free planning** — "we carry the weight so you can be present."
2. **Personalized experience** — "no two couples alike; never templated."
3. **Attention to detail** — "the magic lives in the small things."
4. **Timeless elegance** — classic over trendy.
5. **Meaningful celebrations** — it's a story, not a checklist.
6. **Local expertise** — Charlotte & the Carolinas, trusted vendor relationships.
